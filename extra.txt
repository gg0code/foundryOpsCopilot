"Eight pains that quietly cost crores every year, recognised by every Indian foundry plant head."

Indian foundries — especially automotive/engine casting manufacturers — suffer from:

#	Pain Point	Business Impact

2	Multiple Defect Types	Blow holes, gas porosity, shrinkage, cold shut, misrun, cracks — each with different root causes

4	Seasonal Variation	Monsoon humidity destroys consistency through non-linear moisture-driven porosity
5	OEM Tolerance Pressure	Automotive customers demand Cpk ≥ 1.33 per PPAP — miss it, lose the contract
6	Rework Cost Spiral	Rework often costs more than scrap, yet invisible in most dashboards
7	Customer Complaints & Warranty	One escaped defect can cost ₹4.5L in warranty exposure
8	Energy Waste	Furnaces over-melt "just in case" — costs ₹50L/year per furnace
✅ The Solution
FoundryOps Copilot uses historical process and quality data to predict casting defects before metal is poured — enabling smarter decisions to improve yield, reduce rejection, and protect customer quality. It is engineered for automotive/engine castings and calibrated to IS 210 FG260 grey iron.

Hero Numbers (Current → With AI):
Scrap Rate: 6.8% → 3.5% (FY25 vs AI target)
OEM Complaints: 14/yr → < 3/yr
⭐ Key Features / Operational Capabilities
1. Pre-Pour Defect Prediction
Defect probability surfaced 4–8 minutes before pour, while parameters can still be corrected. Operators move from reacting to scrap to preventing it. Tracked via P(scrap) score per heat.

2. Multi-Class Defect Identification
A 10-class multinomial model names the specific defect type AND uses SHAP attributions to point to the exact root driver (e.g., "cooling rate 14°C/min" or "pattern age 1,100 cycles").

3. Earlier Disposition Decisions (Rework Risk)
Severity classifier separates likely scrap, rework, and good castings at the heat level — stopping marginal castings from entering costly machining loops. Triggers a hold when P(rework) > 25%.

4. Furnace Energy Optimiser
Energy regressor models kWh/ton from process inputs. Recommends the minimum-safe superheat, eliminating the "+30°C just in case" buffer that burns ₹50L/yr in electricity.

5. Warranty Risk Firewall
Composite warranty risk score (0–10) flags heats above 7 for extra QC. Reduces escapes by 40–70% per industry benchmarks. Borderline parts caught before OEM shipment, not at the field.

6. Throughput Improvement
Fewer scrapped pours → fewer mid-shift resets. Earlier disposition → machining centers run cleaner. Anomaly detection surfaces equipment drift before downtime. More good castings per shift — no new capex.

7. Tribal Knowledge Encoding
The model trains on years of heat logs including senior operators' pours — encoding expertise into a model that runs every shift, forever.

8. Seasonal Intelligence
Humidity, sand moisture, and pour delay are features in the model. It learns the non-linear compounding (moisture^1.5 × humidity^1.2) and tightens setpoints in monsoon season proactively.

🤖 AI/ML Stack (Under the Hood)
Tech	Role
XGBoost	Defect prediction classifier — 89.6% accuracy (target ≥ 82%), 10 defect classes, probability output in 50ms
LightGBM	Yield & cost regression — R² 0.680; Warranty risk regression — R² 0.729
